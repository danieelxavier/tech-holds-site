Shore Labor

We offer services on Vessels' Cargo Holds (Cleaning and Preparation) for dry bulk carriers. We’re ready to attend ships at ports as Manaus-AM, Itacoatiara-AM, Santarém-PA and Mosqueiro Pilot Station-PA. Our costs include truck transport, road transport, boat and support boats, besides the whole individual protection equipment, food and drinking water from shore.

Cleaning Team

Our boarding team is specialized in following residues’ removal: metallurgical coal (Hulha Betuminosa), manganese ore, iron ore, bauxite, fertilizer, pig iron, alumina, china clay (caolin), among others. We availabe of 16 men with extensive experience and expertise in cleaning and preparation of cargo holds mainly higher parts, top of the bulkheads, access ladders and frames.

Equipments

Our equipment allow us to provide with satisfactory results in short time. Fresh water is applied using high-pressure water jet machines (400~500 BAR power pressure), for reaching the upper parts, removing all the remains and stains and any losing particles as well.
